COMMAND: Join three tables.

SQL: SELECT reviews.stars, reviews.comment, businesses.name, users.first_name, users.last_name
   ...> FROM reviews
   ...> JOIN businesses ON reviews.business_id = businesses.id
   ...> JOIN users ON reviews.user_id = users.id;

COMMAND: Choose to view just the user's names, the business name, the star rating, and the comment.

SQL: ELECT users.first_name, users.last_name, businesses.name, reviews.stars, reviews.comment
   ...> FROM users

COMMAND: View the data of all three tables for just one particular business.

SQL: SELECT users.first_name, users.last_name, businesses.name, reviews.stars, reviews.comment
   ...> FROM businesses
   ...> JOIN reviews ON reviews.business_id = businesses.id
   ...> JOIN users ON reviews.user_id = users.id
